Who is UGH

Born From Noise. Dressed in Color.

UGH is a visual identity, performance character, and emerging clothing brand built on one principle: nothing should be boring. Every piece, every image, every drop is designed to make you feel something — even if that feeling is confusion.

The character bleeds from performance art into fashion, from street into gallery, from toy chest into wardrobe. If it's loud, layered, and a little unhinged — it's UGH.

Merch is coming soon. Tees, hoodies, and things we haven't named yet. Stay close.
